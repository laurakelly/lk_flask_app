Hi, I'm Laura Kelly. I'm a web developer who loves Python and Django, and I'm a freelance mixed media artist in my spare time. I'm currently a student at Hackbright Academy. Prior to becoming a developer, I studied philosophy and neuroscience at Washington University in St. Louis. After graduating, I worked in a neuroscience lab at UC Davis for two years studing memory with fMRI, EEG, and eye-tracking. I taught myself Python to do data analysis in the lab and liked programming so much that I decided to go into web development.
